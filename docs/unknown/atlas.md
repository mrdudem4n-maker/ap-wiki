It is essential to note that interpretations of the nature and structure of Satan's kingdom may vary among different Christian denominations and traditions. While some emphasize a literal interpretation of fallen angels and demons, others view these concepts more symbolically or metaphorically, representing evil influences in human society or the individual struggle against temptation.
